## How to work through this list efficiently

1. **Open Product Hunt first** — but don't submit yet. Product Hunt has a "launch day" mechanic where all your upvotes stack on ONE day. Plan a real launch for later (Week 3-4), after you have some early users to upvote. Skip for now and come back.

2. **Batch the rest in one sitting (~90 min)**. Have the standard fields above open in a note-taking app, and rotate through each directory: open URL → find "Submit" → paste fields → upload screenshot → submit → check box above.

3. **Track rejections**. Some directories (Indie Hackers, AlternativeTo) are curated and will reject if your submission looks low-effort. For rejected ones, upgrade the description quality and retry in 2 weeks.

4. **Expected result**: out of 30 submissions, ~15-20 will publish. Each gives 1 backlink (DR 30-60 range). That's enough to move your domain authority from ~0 to ~10-15 within 6-8 weeks.

5. **Check back on the Tier-2 AI directories weekly**. Many of them "publish" by adding you to their database but the listing only goes live after a manual review cycle. Once live, screenshot the listing page and save the link — useful social proof later.

## Submission etiquette

- **Don't use the same exact description verbatim on every site**. Google penalizes duplicate content (slightly) and some directories reject obvious copy-paste. Vary the long description by 20-30% for each directory — shuffle sentences, swap synonyms.
- **Never lie about user counts or ratings**. On Product Hunt especially, fake metrics get your launch shadow-banned.
- **Include the Magick Coloring logo/icon as a square 1:1 image** — most directories will crop anything else badly.
- **Category choice matters**. When in doubt, pick "Image Generation" or "Art & Design" (higher-traffic categories than "Productivity").
